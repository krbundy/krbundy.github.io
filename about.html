About me

I was born and raised in a small farming town; we were on the main road through town and could get to a grocery store in about twenty minutes. If you wanted a 'city' feel, that was close to an hour away. From here, I went to the University of Maine, Orono, for my undergraduate and graduate work. I took some time between them to work in tech support and at a hotel; both were interesting jobs but I knew those were temporary positions when I took them (really, they were seasonal).

Until recently, I was teaching as a lecturer at the University of Maine at Augusta. While I learned a lot about interacting with people and collaborating with coworkers from a distance, I realized that teaching was not my passion. Instead, I am pursuing a career in data science. I have always loved technology, mathematics, and learning new things, so data science seems like a natural fit.

I am the type of person who is always learning something new. Right now, that's Apache Spark.

If you are interested in my services or collaborating on a project, please let me know.
